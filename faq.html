FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers on pricing, timelines, and how working together usually goes.

How much does a WordPress fix cost?

Fixes start at $50. Final price depends on the issue. If I take a look and can't resolve it, you don't pay.

How long does it take to build a website?

Simple landing pages often land in about 3–7 days once direction and content are clear. Bigger builds get a scoped timeline up front.

Do you work with clients outside College Station?

Yes. I serve College Station and Bryan locally, and work with remote clients across Texas and beyond.

Can you build a site without WordPress?

Absolutely. Custom HTML/CSS/JS is often faster and cheaper to host when you want a focused site without plugin overhead.

What do I need to provide to get started?

A short note about the problem or goal is enough. Logos, copy drafts, and examples help — but rough ideas work too.

How much does a simple website cost in College Station?

Focused landing pages typically start around $150. Exact pricing depends on pages, forms, and content needs.
