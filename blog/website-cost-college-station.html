How much does a simple website cost in College Station?

If you run a small business in Bryan–College Station, you do not need an agency quote to get online. Here is a realistic range.

Most local owners ask the same first question: what will this cost? The honest answer depends on what “simple” means — but for a focused one-page site with clear services and a working contact path, budgets often start around $150.

What usually fits near the low end

  • One landing page with hero, services, and contact
  • Mobile-friendly layout from the start
  • Basic SEO titles and location language
  • A form or mailto contact option

What pushes the price up

  • Multiple pages or custom copywriting
  • Booking tools, payments, or complex WordPress plugins
  • Migrating an old broken site
  • Brand design from scratch

WordPress fixes are different

If you already have a site and something is broken — forms, layout, plugins — fixes often start at $50. You pay for diagnosis and a clean repair, not a rebuild you do not need.

Local note for College Station & Bryan

Being nearby helps with quick calls and understanding local businesses, but remote delivery works fine across Texas. The goal is the same: a site that loads fast, explains what you do, and makes contact easy.
