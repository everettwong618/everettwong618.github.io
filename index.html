What is MMET?

Manufacturing & Mechanical Engineering Technology sits between pure theory and the shop floor. It's how products get designed, built, tested, and improved — with CAD, materials, mechanics, data, and clear technical communication.

In plain English: I learn how physical systems work, how to document them, and how to make decisions that hold up under real constraints. That same systems mindset shows up in how I build websites.
